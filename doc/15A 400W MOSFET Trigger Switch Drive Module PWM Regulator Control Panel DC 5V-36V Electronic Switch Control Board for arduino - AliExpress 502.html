DC Motor Speed Controller Module DC 5V-36V High Power MOSFET Switch Drive Module 15A Max 30A 0-20KHz for Lamp Brightness Control
Features:
1.High Power Output: Experience the prowess of this MOSFET trigger switch drive module featuring a dual MOS parallel active output, delivering impressive power with a continuous current of 1A and a maximum power of 400W at room temperature. It satisfies the needs of various high-power devices.
2.Wide Voltage Range, PWM Support: With a working voltage range of DC 5V to 36V and compatibility with PWM signals, this PWM regulator control panel offers versatility in controlling devices. It accepts digital signals within the voltage range of DC 3.3V to 20V, making it suitable for use with microcontroller IO ports, PLC interfaces, and other DC power sources.
3.Robust Output Capability: The PWM adjustment electronic switch control board exhibits robust output capability, allowing control of high-power devices such as motors, bulbs, LED strips, DC motors, mini water pumps, solenoid valves, and more. It enables precise control over motor speed and light brightness through PWM input.
4.Infinite Switching Lifespan: Designed for durability, this DC motor speed controller module boasts an unlimited switching lifespan, ensuring long-term reliability and uninterrupted performance.
5.Compact Design, Easy Integration: Measuring just 34x17x12mm (1.34x0.67x0.47inch), this high power MOSFET switch drive module offers a compact form factor, facilitating effortless integration into various applications. Easily achieve control over high-power devices with this versatile and efficient module.
